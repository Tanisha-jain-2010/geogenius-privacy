Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. What We Collect

GeoGenius does not collect any personal data. All game progress, scores, and settings are stored locally on your device using localStorage and never sent to any server.

2. No Account Required

GeoGenius requires no sign-up, login, or personal information of any kind.

3. No Third-Party Tracking

We do not use analytics, advertising networks, or any third-party tracking services.

4. External Resources

GeoGenius loads fonts from Google Fonts and map data from public CDNs (cdnjs, jsdelivr). These requests are subject to their own privacy policies but contain no personal user data.

5. Children

GeoGenius is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.
